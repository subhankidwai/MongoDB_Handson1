Q. Query the collection ""employee"" and list all the documents

A. db.employee.find()

Q. Query the collection ""employee"" and list the employees who are having salary more than 30000

A. db.employee.find({"salary":{$gt:"30000"}})

Q. Query the collection ""employee"" and list the employees who are having experience more than 2 years.

A. db.employee.find({overallExp:{$gt:"2"}})

Q. Query the collection ""employee"" and list the employees who are graduated after 2015 and having experience more than 1 year

A. db.employee.find({$and:[{yearGrad:{$gt:"2015"}},{overallExp:{$gt:"1"}}]})

Q. Query the collection ""employee"" and update the salary of the employee whose salary is greater than 30000 to 25000.

A. db.employee.updateMany({salary:{$gt:"30000"}},{$set:{salary:"25000"}})

Q. Delete all the documents from ""employee"" where last company is Y"

A. db.employee.deleteMany({lastCompany:"Y"})
